How to publish your page

Upload your finished page to your folder in this GitHub repo. No login on the site — your folder name is your identity.

  1. Click Download my page — you get a ZIP. Inside: index.html (your page), project.json (for editing later), and — if you used them — profile.json, avatar.jpg, feed.json, and feed-images/.
  2. On GitHub, open the people/ folder and create a subfolder with your nick, e.g. people/ania/.
  3. Upload everything from the ZIP into that folder (not the repo root!).

That's it — the people list updates automatically and you appear on the team feed. 🎉

Want to change something later? On your published page, click Edit page — the editor opens with your work already loaded. Edit, download again, and replace the files on GitHub.

Tip: set your display name and avatar in Profile — they show up next to your feed posts.
