Hey there, Fluxon Designer. Welcome to Dumpboard.

Remember when the internet felt smaller? When your first website was a MySpace page or a Tumblr blog you built just because you could?

Yeah. Me too....

That's the whole idea here. Today you're building a personal page – and no, it won't swallow your whole year like a portfolio does. This isn't about work. It's about you.

Start with a blank canvas. Drop in photos, YouTube links, stickers. Change the colors, swap the fonts, break things. I don't mind. It's yours.

One rule: make it unapologetically you. Pin the images you keep coming back to, the things that inspire you, the dumb stuff that makes you grin. It's a Dumpboard.

Be wild. Be free. Just us here on GitHub, doing it for the fun of it.

Ready? Go!
