How it works

Build your page in the editor, download a ZIP, and upload it to GitHub. No coding required.

  1. 1

    Set your nick

    Open the editor and enter your nick when asked (e.g. monika.nowak) — no spaces. This becomes your folder name: people/your-nick/.

    Screenshot
  2. 2

    Add media

    At the top, use the grid to add an image or YouTube link. Add a caption if you like — these items go to the shared team feed.

    Screenshot
  3. 3

    Decorate your page

    Use Background, Stickers, Text, and Draw on the canvas below. Click your avatar to set your name and profile photo.

    Screenshot
  4. 4

    Download & unzip

    Click Download (top right). Find the ZIP in your Downloads folder and unzip it (double-click on Mac, Extract All on Windows).

    Tip: rename the unzipped folder to your nick (e.g. monika.nowak) — it makes the next step easier.

    Screenshot
  5. 5

    Open the repo on GitHub

    1. Go to github.com/monika-nowak/design-team-dumpboard
    2. Click the people folder
    Screenshot
  6. 6

    Upload your folder

    1. Click Add file → Upload files
    2. Drag your whole unzipped folder (e.g. monika.nowak) into the upload area
    3. Check the path looks like people/your-nick/index.html — not the repo root
    4. Click Commit changes
    Screenshot
  7. 7

    See it live & update later

    Wait 1–2 minutes, then open your page at …/people/your-nick/ and check the team feed.

    To update: open your page → Edit dump → change things → Download → unzip → upload again to the same folder.
